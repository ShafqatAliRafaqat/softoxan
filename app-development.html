Mobile Development

Our services in Mobile development

iOS App Development

We provide complete services spanning the entire development cycle from requirements analysis, solution design, UI-Design, development to deployment. We understand the problem and propose the solution that solves it. We offer more flexible engagement models that accommodate the client requirements from developing POC, wireframes to the complete end product. We follow agile methodology approach along MVC, MVP, MVVM & other design patterns which best suits the mobile application under development. With years of experience in Obj-C, Swift and Xcode and we have delivered world class iOS applications for iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch with minimal iterations. Our iOS developers have deep knowledge on Apple iOS SDKs, frameworks, App services, media, web tools and even the latest ARKit tool

Android App Development

Android developers at Softoxan are highly adept at designing, building, testing and deployment of android apps. We have immense experience in developing applications using android software development kit (SDK) for native and HTML5 platforms, React, Xamarin, and Ionic Hybrid Frameworks for cross platform. Our development expertise ranges from simple POC(proof of concept) app development to complex application development like location based, AR ,ERP integrated, Data Visualization, Media App, Automation apps etc. We support all major android devices from android Smartphones to tablet and TVs. Our android app development services include consulting, UI/UX design, Development, Project Management, QA, support and maintenance.

HYbrid App Development

We offer cross-platform mobile app development services to iOS, Android and web platforms. Our developers have considerable knowledge in Xamarin, Ionic, React frameworks and other advanced cross platform compatible technologies to build applications matching native quality. Our refined scrum process and execution methodology helps in speedy development and delivery of apps for major platforms to accommodate maximum users. Hybrid app development will not only reduce the development time and cost but on top of that it will also reduce the support and maintenance of technical resources.

Progressive Web apps

As mobile device usage rises, progressive web apps (PWAs) represent a user-friendly way to bridge the gap between websites and mobile apps. We are using modern technology to deliver app-like experiences to users, right in their browsers.
